Quesadillas


Prep Time - 10 mins

Cook Time - 20 mins


Ingredients

  • 2 pounds small new potatoes
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt, plus more
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup unrefined sunflower oil
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped fresh dill

Directions

  1. Measure 2 pounds new potatoes: If they’re about an inch in diameter, leave them whole. If they’re larger, cut into 1-inch chunks, making sure all the potatoes are roughly the same size to ensure even cooking.

  2. Place the potatoes in a large pot and add enough water to cover by about 2 inches. Add 2 teaspoons kosher salt, cover, and bring to a boil over medium-high heat.

  3. Reduce the heat to low and gently simmer with the lid ajar until the potatoes can be pierced easily with a fork, being careful not to overcook them, 12 to 15 minutes.

  4. Meanwhile, finely grate 3 garlic cloves or run through a garlic press and place in a small bowl. Add 1/4 cup sunflower oil and stir to combine. Finely chop fresh dill until you have 1/2 cup.

  5. Drain the potatoes and return to the pot. Add the garlic oil, dill, and a pinch of kosher salt. Gently toss to combine. Taste and season with more salt as needed.
