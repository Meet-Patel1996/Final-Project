Teriyaki Skirt Steak


Description


Great dinner that can be created to meal prep for an entire week.


Prep Time - 1 hour

Cook Time - 20 mins


Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds skirt steak
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon lime juice
  • ½ cup kosher olive oil
  • ¼ cup kosher teriyaki sauce
  • ¼ cup kosher Worcestershire sauce

Directions

  1. Mix marinade ingredients together.

  2. Place steak in a Ziploc bag and pour in marinade. Allow steak to marinate for 1 hour in the fridge.

  3. Preheat grill / cast iron pan to medium high heat. Cook steak for 6-7 minutes on each side. Once done cooking allow steak to rest for 10 minutes.

  4. Slice steak against the grain and enjoy!
