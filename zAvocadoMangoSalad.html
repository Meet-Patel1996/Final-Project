Avocado Mango Salad


Prep Time - 5 min


Ingredients

  • 2 Romaine Lettuce Hearts, Chopped
  • 11 avocado, Cut into small pieces
  • 1 mango, cut into small pieces
  • ½ red onion diced/li>

Dressing

  • ¼ cup canola oil
  • ¼ cup kosher honey
  • 3 tablespoons mustard
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • ¼ cup kosher light mayonnaise

Directions

  1. Mix dressing ingredients in a small bowl.

  2. Mix salad ingredients in a medium bowl and pour over dressing. Mix and enjoy!
