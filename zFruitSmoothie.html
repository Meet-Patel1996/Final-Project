Fruit Smoothie


Description


Easy and versatile breakfast that has many different combinations to your liking.


Prep Time - 5 min


Ingredients

  • 1 cup vanilla yogurt
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 1 cup kosher almond milk
  • 2 cups frozen fruit chunks of your liking

Directions

  1. Place all ingredients into a strong blender and blend until smooth. Once smooth blend another full minute.

  2. If its too thick add more milk or orange juice to reach consistency that you like.
