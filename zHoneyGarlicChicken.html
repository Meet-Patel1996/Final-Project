Honey Garlic Chicken


Description


Simple and delicious honey garlic chicken that’s easy to make and budget friendly.


Prep Time - 10 min

Cook Time - 50 mins


Ingredients

  • 4 Chicken Leg quarter, thighs and drums separated
  • ½ cup kosher honey
  • 4 cloves of fresh garlic
  • 2 scallions
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • ¼ teaspoon pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 Degrees Fahrenheit

  2. Wash and dry chicken and place in a pan.

  3. In a bowl combine honey, garlic, scallions, salt, and pepper. Mix and pour the mixture evenly over the chicken.

  4. Bake uncovered for one hour.
